RedisInsight

RedisInsight is a graphical tool for visualizing and optimizing data in Redis. While not required for this course, we strongly recommend that you download a free copy of RedisInsight and connect it to your Redis instance.

The RedisInsight key browser has specialized views for inspecting the contents of Streams and monitoring the state of Consumer Groups.
